Website Process

Step 1 - Strategy
We'll meet for a strategic meeting to discuss your goals, brand, and essential features. From this, we develop a comprehensive website strategy and design inspiration.
(40% of the payment is required to initiate this step)

Step 2 - Design
Using the strategic brief, we create a custom website design that is visually stunning, fast-loading, easy to maintain, and user-centric.

Step 3 - Development
Once you approve the design, we build the site from scratch using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. This includes comprehensive browser testing, speed testing, and optimization.
(50% of the payment is required to initiate this step)

Step 4 - Review & Upload Content
We review the live site on a hidden domain, upload all your content, and train you on the content management system for easy ongoing management.
(10% of the payment is required to initiate this step)

Step 5 - Site Live
After final approval, we push the site live on your domain and conduct a final round of testing.
