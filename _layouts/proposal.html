Website Process

Step 1 - Strategy
We engaged in a strategic meeting with relevant key parties to discuss goals, the brand, important features and functionality, etc. We'll then go away and come away with a comprehensive website strategy, and design inspiration.
(40% of the payment is required to initiate this step)

Step 2 - Design
Based on the strategic brief, we'll come up with a custom design for the website. This design will be optimized not just to be visually stunning, but to load fast, be easy to update and maintain and planned with an understanding of the psychology of the user.

Step 3 - Development
After you have reviewed and approved the design we'll get to work building it. We'll be building from scratch using HTML, CSS and Javascript (no clunky pre-built templates here). We'll conduct comprehensive browser testing, speed testing and website optimisation.
(50% of the payment is required to initiate this step)

Step 4 - Review & Upload Content
Now it's time to review the live site (on a hidden domain), upload all of your content (photos, images etc) and teach you how to use the content management system so that it's easy for you to manage ongoing.
(10% of the payment is required to initiate this step)

Step 5 - Site Live
When all has been signed off, we'll push the site live on the client name domain and conduct one final round of testing.
